Gmagick::blurimage

(PECL gmagick >= Unknown)

Gmagick::blurimage — Adds blur filter to image



说明

public Gmagick::blurimage(float $radius, float $sigma, int $channel = ?): Gmagick

Adds blur filter to image.



参数

Blur radius

返回值
