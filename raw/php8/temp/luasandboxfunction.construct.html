LuaSandboxFunction::__construct

(PECL luasandbox >= 1.0.0)

LuaSandboxFunction::__construct — Unused



说明

final private LuaSandboxFunction::__construct()

{@link LuaSandboxFunction} are obtained as a return value from Lua, as a parameter passed to a callback from Lua, or by using {@link LuaSandbox::wrapPhpFunction()}, {@link LuaSandbox::loadString()}, or {@link LuaSandbox::loadBinary()}. They cannot be constructed directly.



参数

此函数没有参数。
