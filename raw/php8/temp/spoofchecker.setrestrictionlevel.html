Spoofchecker::setRestrictionLevel

(PHP 7 >= 7.3.0, PHP 8)

Spoofchecker::setRestrictionLevel — Set the restriction level



说明

public Spoofchecker::setRestrictionLevel(int $level): void

Sets the restriction level of {@link SpoofChecker::isSuspicious()}.



参数

level
The restriction level of {@link SpoofChecker::isSuspicious()}. One of Spoofchecker::ASCII, Spoofchecker::SINGLE_SCRIPT_RESTRICTIVE, Spoofchecker::HIGHLY_RESTRICTIVE, Spoofchecker::MODERATELY_RESTRICTIVE, Spoofchecker::MINIMALLY_RESTRICTIVE, or Spoofchecker::UNRESTRICTIVE. Defaults to Spoofchecker::HIGHLY_RESTRICTIVE.


返回值

没有返回值。
