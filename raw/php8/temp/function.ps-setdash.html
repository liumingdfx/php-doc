ps_setdash

(PECL ps >= 1.1.0)

ps_setdash — Sets appearance of a dashed line



说明

ps_setdash(resource $psdoc, float $on, float $off): bool

Sets the length of the black and white portions of a dashed line.



参数

psdoc

Resource identifier of the postscript file as returned by {@link ps_new()}.

on

The length of the dash.

off

The length of the gap between dashes.



返回值

成功时返回 true， 或者在失败时返回 false。



参见

  • {@link ps_setpolydash()} - Sets appearance of a dashed line
